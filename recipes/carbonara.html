Carbonara Sauce

Description

Very simple and delicious italian sauce based on the traditional "cacio e pepe" sauce, but with the addition of egg yolks and bacon or pancetta.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2 per person)
  • Parmesan or Percorino
  • Bacon or Pancetta
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. You can start by boiling the pasta water while you do the other steps of the recipe.
  2. While the water is getting hot, you should break 2 eggs per person, keep the egg yolks and throw the egg whites away. Mix the egg yolks with some grated parmesan or pecorino until they get mixed together as one.
  3. Cut the bacon or pancetta in little cubes and fry them in a pan until they are brown.
  4. When the pasta water is boiling, cook the pasta al dente, mix the pasta with a little bit of the pasta water with the bacon, and then mix the egg+parmesan mix, pay attention to keep the temperature low enough so the eggs don`t cook.
